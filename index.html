What is Virtual Biomedical Lab?

The Virtual Biomedical Laboratory is an interactive e-learning platform where students, healthcare professionals, and enthusiasts can explore cardiovascular and respiratory physiology through simulations of ECG, SpO₂, and Spirometry.
